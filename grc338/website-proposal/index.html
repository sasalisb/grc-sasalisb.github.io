Introduction

Prismo Playground

Prismo Playground is in the Music/Events/Entertainment industry specifically Music Festivals. Prismo Playground is a two-weekend music festival located on pismo beach. This kaleidoscope and rainbow themed night beach party is one of the most exciting events of the school year and a great way to kick off summer in this college town.

Target audience

Prismo Playground's target audience are college students, music lovers, and party people!

These people are seeking vibrant and lively activity to brighten up their life and let loose. Their primary tasks and goals when visiting the site are to purchase passes, discover the artist line-up, and learn more about the event.
