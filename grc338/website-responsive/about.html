About

Prismo Playground is the event of the year and the best way to kick off the summer. If you're seeking vibrant and lively opprotunities to brighten up your life and let loose this weekend festival is for you! Join us in the party!

Prismo Playground was started by four students in GrC 402 for their group project. Prismo Playground is a ficticious event in which their ficticous marketing agency, "SLAM Marketing" developed a marketing campaign inccorporating variable data in hopes of getting an A.
